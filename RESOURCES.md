## Design Principles

### UI/UX Best Practices
- **[10 UI Design Principles](https://x.com/kieranklaassen/status/1992350089118183433)** - Fewer colors, larger fonts, more whitespace, system fonts, high contrast
- **[Avoiding AI Slop Design](https://x.com/mengto/status/1992213336205951143)** - Study Swiss design (Dieter Rams), Japanese minimalism (Muji), Apple's design system

### Design Philosophy
- **Precise and Weighty** - Create digital interfaces that feel substantial and tactile
- **Physical Material Inspiration** - Fine leather grain, wood furniture texture, weighted mechanical movements
- **Luxury Dashboard References** - Ferrari/Lamborghini/Rolls Royce instrument clusters

## Notes

**Design Direction:**
- Avoid AI aesthetic clich√©s (purple gradients, glows, glass morphism)
- Study timeless design: Braun, Muji, Apple
- System fonts, heavy weights, large sizes, high contrast
- Generous whitespace, minimal shadows
- Reduce abstraction - make UI elements feel physical and purposeful

**Core Principle:**
Create interfaces that feel like precision instruments, not floating digital abstractions.
